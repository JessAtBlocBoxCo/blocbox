Become a Blocbox Host

Blocbox quickly and easily allows you to find nearby neighbors acting as Blocbox hosts, arrange for your packages to be delivered to them, and be notified so you can conveniently pick up your package when you get home, or whenever you like.

PERFORM TASKS WHILE AT HOME

Offer any service you can think of to your neighbors. Hosts must be able to be verified and be at home for all scheduled deliveries.

MEET YOUR NEIGHBORS

Set and arrange times at which your neighbors can pick up their packages at your door. Hosts must be home a minimum amount of hours in the evening to allow neighbors to pick up their packages.
